Reincarnation

What is Reincarnation? Reincarnation is what is truly believed by several religions (except Buddhism, which is not considered to be a religion); therefore, the following information includes Buddhism because its members believe and teach reincarnation. Not only Buddhism and religions believe and teach it, but also philosophy and mythology expound its theory and practice of teaching the transcendental experience of our returning to live again in another life, which includes esotericism and metaphysics. In addition, Hinduism and Sikhism teach and believe in reincarnation, whereby, Hinduism believes that when we die we either go to heaven or hell according to the way we lived; however, Sikhism does not believe we go to heaven or hell when we die, in fact, Sikhism teaches and believes that we live a hell in earth by suffering the consequences of not living according Sikhism doctrine, on the other hand, we live a life of spiritual harmony and peace if we do live according to its doctrine, including the Greek Philosophers Pythagoras, Plato, Socrates, Aristotle taught and believed in Reincarnation. In addition, Reincarnation is taught by Spiritism, Theosophy, and the Rosicrucians.

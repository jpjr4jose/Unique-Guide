In fact, everything in the universe, including our bodies and our thoughts, are both corpuscles and wavelengths of high frequencies.

Once we replace our recognition of the theories of, “selfconsciousness and subconciousness,” with the reality of collective consciousness we will become aware of its knowledge and its many esoteric aspects and spiritual revelations.
